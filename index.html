What Is Blackjack?

Blackjack is a popular casino card game played between a dealer and one or more players. The game involves trying to get a higher total point value than the dealer, without exceeding 21. Each player is dealt two cards, and they can choose to hit (draw another card) or stand (keep their current hand). The dealer must hit until their hand reaches a value of 17 or more. The game combines skill and luck, making it a favorite among casino-goers.
